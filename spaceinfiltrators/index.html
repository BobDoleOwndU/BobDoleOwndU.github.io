What is Space Infiltrators?

Space Infiltrators is a horizontal JavaScript remake of the classic game Space Invaders.

Space Infiltrators was designed and created by BobDoleOwndU in about a week.

All assets except sound effects were created by BobDoleOwndU.

Note: A newer more well sized version of this game is in development.

Q: What's the highest score ever acheived on this game?

A: The highest score ever acheived was by myself at 49934 points. I've not come anywhere close to that score since I earned it. If you have beaten this score (with proof), tell me and I'll update this!
